# Kitchen Cabinet Standards

Reference dimensions from Home Depot and industry standards.

## Base Cabinets

Base cabinets sit on the floor and support countertops.

- **Height**: 34.5" (36" with countertop)
- **Depth**: 24" standard
- **Countertop overhang**: ~1" beyond cabinet face
- **Toe kick**: 3.5-4.5" at bottom
- **Common widths**: 12", 15", 18", 21", 24", 27", 30", 33", 36", 42", 48"
  - Available in 3" increments

## Wall Cabinets

Wall cabinets hang above countertops.

- **Height options**: 12", 15", 18", 24", 30", 36", 42"
  - Most common: 30", 36", or 42"
- **Depth**: 12" standard, 24" for special purposes
- **Mounting**: Bottom 18" above countertop (54" from floor)
- **Common widths**: 9", 12", 15", 18", 21", 24", 27", 30", 33", 36"
  - Available in 3" increments

## Tall/Pantry Cabinets

Floor-to-ceiling storage cabinets.

- **Height**: 84", 90", or 96"
- **Depth**: 12" or 24"
- **Widths**: 12", 18", 24", 30", 36"

## Layout Notes

- **Wall cabinets to countertop**: 18" gap
- **Base cabinet alignment**: Match appliance depth (24")
- **Island base cabinets**: Can be deeper (up to 30") for more storage
- **Upper cabinet clearance**: Choose height based on ceiling height
